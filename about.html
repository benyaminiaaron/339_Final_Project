About Us

Welcome to RentMe.com, a website designed for college students to sublet their rooms and houses to other students on campus. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use platform that connects students who are looking for affordable and convenient housing options with other students who are looking to sublet their living spaces.


Our website is specifically tailored to college campuses, allowing students to post their sublets in a safe and secure environment. On our Post page, students can fill out a form to submit their sublet information and photos, which will then be reviewed by our team before being published on the website. We take safety and security seriously and strive to ensure that all listings are legitimate and accurate.


Thank you for choosing RentMe.com for your subletting needs. We hope you find our website helpful and convenient.
